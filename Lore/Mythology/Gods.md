The gods used to be amongst the people before the continent. In the war that [[Sundering of Taeltoranou|shattered the continent]], some of the gods betrayed the others and unleashed atrocities upon the continent. Only the combined strength of the united gods was able to seal the betrayers, and in turn they had to seal themselves away. In doing so, the continent was shattered. Before they did so, they left behind marks on heroes and leaders, to indicate who would be most worthy of leading them. Their descendents have a birthmark of their respective deity, as well as divine sorcerous powers, indicating their worthiness to continue ruling
