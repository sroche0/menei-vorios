The continent the campaign is taking place on. The setting takes place roughly 800 years after a [[Sundering of Taeltoranou|cataclysmic event]] that shatter the continent into 3, which are unable to contact each other. The continent is dangerous to travel, and you would likely travel in groups, as you did in the first game. Additionally, the remnants of civilization mostly congregated into denser urban areas.
