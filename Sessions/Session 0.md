There was an attack on the camp by [[darklings]]. We were able to drive them off but they were able to grab the egg that had been recently unearthed. Three archaeologists were hurt in the attack before we drove off the [[darklings]]. Unfortunately one of them died before i was able to stabilize them.

[[master gren]] summoned us to their tent afterward and tasked us with tracking down the darklings. They offered 25g each for retreiving the egg and gave us a requisition order to get supplies from the quartermaster. [[Eltimaine]] continues to terrorize this poor assistant

We went to talk to some of the scouts of the expeditionto try to get a lay of the land around the dig site. They didnt have any maps drawn up yet but we hired on [[Ivandra]] to come with us for a mere 2gp thanks to [[Kentucky Black|Kentucky's]] negotiations

We follow a squirell that [[Crimini]] and [[Grunabel]] had convinced to help us. The squirell tells us about a structure in the woods that the darklings came from. We repay it with some acorns. We are able to navigate to the structure the squirrel described

The structure is a shrine atop a small hill. The shrine is nondescript and for an unknown god. The architecture suggests that it is Fae in origin. There are no lights or fires from up ahead at the [[shrine]]. We decide to place [[Grunabel]] and [[Raya]] in the middle of the formation as they cant see well and fjord the river to approach the shrine. There is the main structure of the shrine and off the side, a storehouse.

We approach the storehouse which is locked. [[Crimini]] manages to pick the lock with ease. There are suspiciously good rations for a camp of [[darklings]]. We move on into the shrine and ar ambushed by [[darklings]]

### Combat

* I cast bless on [[Crimini]], [[Kentucky Black]], and [[Raya]]

After killing the last of the [[darklings]] we take a moment to catch our breath with a short rest and look around the shrine. Some of us recognize the statues as being [[eldron]] that are wearing the garb of the fey city of [[Sendrion]]

Immediately in front of us when we go through the door in the back is a statue of a nymph looking creature in front of a mural. There is an inscription on the base of the statue that says "Call twice for the [[Alseed]], once for the [[Lumpad]], and once for the [[Naiad]]" The statue has a gong hanging from its hand.

There is a sarcophagus on the side of the room that we manage to pry open. Within is a skeleton holding a pristine longsword +1 and a peg that looks about the size of the holes in the mural with a "meeple" on the end

We insert the peg in turn into slots that match the alseed, lumpad, and naiad and ring the gong the appropriate amount of times. The mural descends with a grinding sound revealing a chamber and descending path. We move into the room and the mural raises back up behind us. There is a door on the other side of the revealed room

[[Eltimaine]] detects necromantic energy on the other side of the door. We open it and see a few darklings in front of a nymph like statues holding the egg we are looking for. Off to the side there is a large wolflike creature with a vine coming out of it. There are also several twig like creatures in front of it

It was a tough fight where multiple of us were downed but we managed to disrupt the ritual the darklings were trying perform and return to the dig site with the egg and 1 darkling shaman in captivity. Ivandra was no longer outside where we left
